Customer Loyalty and Market Basket Analysis for 2019

Customer loyalty is an ongoing positive relationship between a customer and a business. It’s what drives repeat purchases and prompts existing customers to choose your company over a competitor offering similar benefits. Therefore it is important to analyse how many of the old ones are keep coming to your business. Are they satisfied enough from your service or product? What percentage of your revenue is from them?
